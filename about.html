About me:

Who am I?

Born and raised on the sunny Isle of Wight (a small island off the south coast of England) I am a 26 year old final year computer science undergraduate student at Goldsmiths College, University of London. Next year I will be starting my PhD at Goldsmiths as part of the IGGI programme.

Since first picking up a keyboard to code in anger I have fallen in love with programming and problem solving. Prior coming to Goldsmiths I worked for several years in Video Game Development and so have experience working both independently and as part of a team.
