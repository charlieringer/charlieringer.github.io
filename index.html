Who am I?

I am a PhD student at Goldsmiths, University of London (but based in York), as part of the IGGI Doctoral Training Center (find out more here). I am currently active in AI and Machine Learning applications for Games research with a focus on the ways we can use Deep Learning to model various facets of video games streams.

What do I do?

  • I solve problems using Machine Learning, especially focusing on Deep Learning and multi-view data. I do this using Keras and Python.
  • I also have an general interest in other approaches to Artificial Intelligence e.g. Video Game AI and Evolutionary Algorithms.
  • I design and make games, normally developing in Unity, I am espically interested games with a focus on novel AI and/or interactions with AI.
  • I enjoy giving talks and (informally) teaching people about Machine Learning and AI.

Interested?

If you are interested in me or my work I would love to hear from you. I am interested in freelance work or consultancy as well as discussing ideas and collaborating on projects. Using one of the links below to get in touch.
