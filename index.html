Who am I?

I am a PhD student at Goldsmiths, University of London (but based in York), as part of the IGGI Doctoral Training Center (find out more here). I am currently active in AI and Machine Learning applications for Games research with a focus on the ways we can use Deep Learning to model various facets of video games streams.

What do I do?

  • I design and implement Machine Learning algorithms, especially focusing on Deep Learning on multi-view data. I do this using Keras and Python.
  • I design and make games, normally in develop in Unity, espically games with interesting AI.
  • I enjoy giving talks and (informally) teaching people and Machine Learning and AI.
  • I also have an interest in other approaches to Artificial Intelligence e.g. Video Game AI and Evolutionary Algorithms.
